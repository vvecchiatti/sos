2024 Season Roundup

2024 · Söuthsea Swimrun

With the season having come to an end in the UK, here is a round up of Söuthsea Swimrun's racing activities from 2024. Hopefully this might inspire a few of you to join in for your first swimruns next year. Just look how beautiful this sport of ours is.

May — EX Swimrun, Sweden

The year kicked off with Anthony, Mark and Sarah heading to Sweden for the EX Swimrun — a friendly two-day event around Edsviken in Sollentuna, easily accessible from central Stockholm. Mark took the 14km and Anthony and Sarah the 24km. Saturday's supersprint knock-out races showcased world-class athletes. Sunday's races culminated in an adventurous cliff jump, just to liven things up.

June — Studland

Adrian and Sarah took part in the inaugural Studland Swimrun hosted by iSwimrun — multiple distances, glorious coastal scenery, and a field full of UK swimrun's great and good alongside plenty of first-timers.

July — Mad Hatters Hokey Cokey Roseland

Sarah took on the full 36km at Mad Hatters Hokey Cokey Roseland in Cornwall. After a mild sense of humour failure in the longest swim, she landed second on the ladies podium. A gorgeous, tough, deeply fulfilling course.

August — Isles of Scilly & The Gribben

Sarah joined the first Isles of Scilly Swimrun — an archipelago adventure around eight islands. Second on the solo ladies podium. Always the bridesmaid...

A week later, Sarah, Anthony, Adrian, Louise and Lulu reconvened in Fowey for The Gribben. Sadly, Anthony took a tumble. But Sarah and Lulu had a wonderful day and, through no fault of their own, finally took the top step of the podium.

October — Ötillö Cannes

Sarah wrapped up the season at the Ötillö Cannes World Series — 32km running and 8km swimming across 22 sections around the French Riviera. Warm seas, stunning scenery, and a reminder of why this sport is unlike anything else.
